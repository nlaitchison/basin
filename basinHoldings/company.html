Basin Tools

Basin Tools is a portfolio of equipment manufacturers providing advanced oilfield equipment and technology to North American and international customers. Whether start-ups or acquisitions, our manufacturing companies run independently, and are therefore flexible and focused on delivering world class products at competitive prices. To find out more visit the Basin Tools site here.

Wenzel Downhole Tools

Wenzel Downhole Tools is a leading motor and drilling tool supplier, with worldwide operations supporting the oilfield, construction, and environmental industries. Wenzel’s reputation for quality is upheld through excellence in design, expertise and customer focus. Wenzel products are manufactured in Edmonton, Canada – an ISO and API certified facility. Wenzel’s operations worldwide spread across the US, Europe, Latin America and Middle East.
