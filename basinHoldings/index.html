About Basin Holdings

Basin Holdings is a global, independent, and integrated oilfield supply & service company with operating businesses in four key oifield sectors: Specialty Equipment Manufacturing (Basin Tools), Diversified Services (Basin Energy Services), Rentals (Basin Rentals), and Equipment Supply and Distribution (Basin Supply). Our global reach into the emerging hydrocarbon markets is an important differentiator between Basin and the competition. We currently manufacture oilfield equipment in North America, and distributeequipment and supporting services through our global network of procurement centers, sales offices, and on-site supply stores. We have locations in North America, Africa, the Former Soviet Union, India, Asia, the Middle East, and Latin America. Basin Holdings employs over 700 people across 16 countries, and we are committed to providing the industry’s most advanced technologies and best customer service to companies, large or small, in every drilling market in the world.
