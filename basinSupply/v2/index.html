Basin Supply

A Global Integrated Oil Supply Company

Basin Supply serves customers in the emerging and developed oilfield markets, providing integrated equipment sales & distribution, procurement, and logistics management around the globe. With 40 years of industry experience, we specialize in supplying Maintenance, Repair and Operations products (MRO), Oil Country Tubular Goods (OCTG), and capital items such as drilling, production, and workover equipment. Our global presence, experience, and network offer our customers and affiliates a unique sales, distribution, and procurement channel for oilfield products and services in global markets with difficult barriers to access. Basin Supply offices are strategically located with the fundamental goal of providing the products our customers need and the support they demand, anywhere in the world.

Equipment Supply
& On-site Stores

We are experienced in setting up on-site stocking locations and distribution centers that offer our customers the ability to access supplies and equipment at their own facilities. We order and manage the inventory, staff the location with trained personnel, and manage all of the necessary paperwork. Our sales office locations include the UAE, Oman, Kuwait, Russia, the USA, Canada, Peru, Colombia, Nigeria, Kenya, and India.

Procurement
& Sourcing

Basin Supply procures products through our chain of stores, warehouses, and international procurement teams in the United States, Canada, China, and Russia. We provide a procurement and distribution channel between the developed and emerging oilfield markets and we handle all the logistics to deliver products on-time and on-budge. We are not brokers, but an experienced supply company with deep vendor relationships.

Logistics Managment
& Customer Service

We have local offices around the world and dedicated company employees who manage our customers logistics needs for “door-to-door” product delivery in every oilfield market. Our local presence provides stability in international supply deals and helps mitigate problems before they arise.
